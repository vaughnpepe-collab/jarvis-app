Questions

Do my tenants have to download anything?

No. They text a normal phone number like they'd text you. There's nothing for them to install or learn — it just feels like fast, friendly service.

Will it spend my money without asking?

Never. Tenly troubleshoots and triages on its own, but anything that costs money — a contractor call-out, a repair — needs your one-tap approval first.

What happens in a real emergency?

Genuine emergencies (flooding, gas, no heat in winter) escalate to you immediately by call and text, while Tenly keeps the tenant calm and informed.

I only have a couple of units. Is this overkill?

That's exactly who Tenly is for. Big operators have staff for this; independent landlords don't. Per-unit pricing means it's affordable even for one or two doors.
