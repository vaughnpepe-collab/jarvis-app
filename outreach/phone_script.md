# Launchly — Cold Call Script

---

## BEFORE YOU CALL

- Know the business name and what they do
- Check if they have a website (Google it + look at Google Maps listing)
- Note something specific: "you're on Checkatrade but no site" / "your current site looks broken on mobile"
- Have the relevant demo link ready to text over immediately after

Best times to call:
- **Trades:** 8:30–10am or 1–3pm, Tuesday–Thursday
- **Restaurants:** 2:30–4:30pm (between lunch and dinner service), Tuesday–Thursday
- **Salons:** 10am–12pm or 2–4pm, Tuesday–Thursday

---

## THE OPENING (first 10 seconds — natural, not scripted)

> "Hi, is that [Business Name]? Great — my name's [Your Name], I build websites for businesses. Very quick — I noticed [Business Name] doesn't have a website / your website looks like it could do with a refresh. Is that something you'd be open to a quick chat about?"

*(Pause. Let them respond. Don't rush.)*

---

## IF THEY SAY YES / "TELL ME MORE"

> "Brilliant. So I build professional websites — fast. The thing that's a bit different about what I do is that I turn them around in 48 hours, and it's a flat £499 with no ongoing fees. No agency, no contracts — just a proper site that looks great and gets people calling you.

> I can actually text you a link right now to a demo site I built for another [plumber / restaurant / salon] — would that help?"

*(If yes → text the demo link immediately, while they're on the phone)*

> "I'll text it over now. And the other thing I offer is a free mockup — I'll build a quick version for [Business Name] specifically, so you can see exactly what yours would look like before you commit to anything. No cost for that. Want me to do that?"

---

## IF THEY SAY "HOW MUCH?"

> "It's £499 all in — one flat fee. You get up to 6 pages, mobile-friendly design, contact form, maps, SEO setup. Everything a proper business website needs. No monthly fees unless you want me to host and maintain it, which is £39 a month and totally optional.

> Most of my clients find it pays for itself in the first month — one or two extra customers from Google more than covers it."

---

## IF THEY SAY "WE ALREADY HAVE A WEBSITE"

> "Of course, sorry — have you got it to hand? I ask because sometimes sites look fine on desktop but have issues on mobile, or aren't ranking as well as they could on Google. I'm not saying yours has problems — but if you want, I can take a look and give you honest feedback. Free, no obligation."

*(This often opens a door — most small business sites have real issues)*

---

## IF THEY SAY "NOT INTERESTED"

> "No problem at all — thanks for taking the call. If you ever want a second opinion on your online presence, feel free to give me a ring. Have a great day."

*(Hang up cleanly. Don't push. Move on immediately.)*

---

## IF THEY SAY "SEND ME SOME INFO"

> "Will do. What's the best email / WhatsApp? I'll send over a demo site and we can go from there — takes 30 seconds to have a look."

*(Send the demo link + Email Template 3 immediately — strike while it's warm)*

---

## CLOSING / NEXT STEPS

> "So the easiest thing is I put together a free mockup for your business — takes me about an hour and you can see exactly what the finished site would look like. If you like it, we agree the price and I build the real thing. If you don't, no hard feelings. Sound fair?"

*(This is the close. A "yes" to a free mockup is a very likely sale.)*

---

## TIPS

- **Aim for 15–20 calls per day** in the first week
- **Expect rejection** — 1 in 10 will want to talk. That's normal. 100 calls = 10 conversations = 2–3 sales = £1,000–£1,500
- **Text the demo link immediately** when they ask — the moment passes quickly
- **The mockup offer is your ace** — nearly everyone who agrees to a free mockup ends up buying
- **Voice matters** — sound confident and curious, not salesy. You're a local business person helping another local business person.
