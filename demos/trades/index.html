FAQ

Common questions.

The ones we get asked most often.

Do you charge a call-out fee? +
No. We don't charge a call-out fee for standard jobs — you only pay for the labour and any parts needed. For emergency out-of-hours callouts, there may be a small uplift, but we'll always tell you upfront before we come.
How quickly can you get to me? +
For emergencies, we aim to be with you within 90 minutes. For non-urgent work, we typically book within 1–3 days. We'll always give you a specific arrival window, not a vague "morning or afternoon" slot.
Are your engineers Gas Safe registered? +
Yes — all our engineers are Gas Safe registered and carry their ID at all times. You can verify our registration at gassaferegister.co.uk. We'll never send an unregistered engineer to work on gas appliances.
Do you provide written quotes? +
Yes, for any job over £150 we provide a written quote before work starts. For smaller jobs, we'll give you a verbal price upfront that we stick to. We don't do "time and materials" unless the job is genuinely exploratory and we agree it with you first.
What guarantee do you offer? +
All labour comes with a 12-month guarantee. Parts are covered by manufacturer warranty (typically 1–5 years). If anything we've installed develops a fault related to our work within 12 months, we'll come back and sort it at no charge.
