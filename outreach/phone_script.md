# Phone / Cold Call Script

## Before You Call
- Find the business on Google Maps
- Note whether they have a website (and if so, how bad it is)
- Find their phone number
- Know their business name and what they do

---

## The Call

**Opening (first 10 seconds — be natural, not robotic):**

> "Hi, is that [Business Name]? Great — my name's [Your Name], I'm a web designer based locally in High Wycombe. I'll keep this very quick — I noticed your business doesn't have a website / your website looks like it could do with a refresh, and I wanted to reach out because I build sites for local businesses in the area. Is that something you'd be open to having a quick chat about?"

---

## If They Say YES / "Tell Me More"

> "Brilliant. So basically I build professional websites for businesses like yours — the kind of site that shows up on Google, looks great on phones, and actually gets people calling you. I work locally so we can meet in person if that helps. Prices start from £399 for a straightforward site, and I can usually have it live within a week. Can I ask — do you get many customers finding you online at the moment, or mostly word of mouth?"

*(Listen — this tells you how to pitch the value)*

> "That's what I thought. A good website would really change that. What I'd suggest is I put together a free mockup of what your site could look like — no cost, no obligation — and if you like it we go from there. Would that work for you?"

---

## If They Say "Not Interested" or "We Already Have One"

> "No problem at all — I appreciate you taking the call. If you ever feel like the site could do with a refresh, or you want a second opinion, feel free to give me a ring. Have a good day."

*(Hang up cleanly. Don't push. Move on.)*

---

## If They Say "How Much?"

> "It depends what you need. A simple 5-page site — who you are, what you do, contact form — starts at £399. A fuller site with a gallery, SEO, Google Business setup is £699. And if you want something more advanced with booking or a menu, that's £1,199. All in — no surprises. I can send you over a price list if that helps?"

---

## If They Say "We're Busy / Call Back Later"

> "Of course — when would be a good time to call back?"

*(Get a specific time. Set a reminder. Call back at that exact time.)*

---

## Closing

> "Brilliant. I'll send you over a link to my portfolio so you can see the kind of work I do, and we'll speak [day/time]. Thanks for your time — speak soon."

---

## Tips

- Aim for 20 calls per day in the first week
- Best times to call: 9–11am or 2–4pm on Tuesday–Thursday
- Don't read the script word for word — know it, then be natural
- Rejection is normal. 1 in 10 calls will lead to a conversation. 1 in 5 of those will convert.
- Log every call: business name, outcome, follow-up date
