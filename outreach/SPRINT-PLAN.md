| **One** mid-tier site | £699 | 70% there in one sale |

**You need 1–2 clients. That's it.** The phone script says 1 in 10 calls becomes a conversation,
1 in 5 of those converts. So ~50 real calls ≈ 1 sale. Two sales ≈ 100 calls. That's **~15 calls a day.**
Achievable. Boring. Real.

## The killer move: the FREE MOCKUP

Don't sell a website. Sell a **free, no-obligation mockup of their actual business.**
- It's the easiest "yes" in the world ("can I show you something free?")
- Once they see *their* name on a clean site, the £399 feels small
- You already have two demos (`demos/restaurant`, `demos/trades`) — swap in their logo/photos/colours and you've got a mockup in ~30 mins

This converts 3–4× better than "do you want a website."

## Day-by-day

### Day 1 — Build the list (don't call yet)
- Google Maps: "restaurants High Wycombe", "plumbers High Wycombe", "salons", "garages", "dentists", "cafes"
- Log **40 businesses** in `lead-tracker.csv` — prioritise ones with **NO website** or an obviously bad one
- Note phone + whether they have a site. That's your week's ammunition.

### Day 2 — First 15 calls + 10 emails
- Calls 9–11am & 2–4pm (best answer rates), use `phone_script.md`
- Email the rest using `email_templates.md` (Template 1 = no site, Template 2 = bad site)
- **Goal: 2–3 "send me a mockup" yeses.** Log every outcome.

### Day 3 — Build mockups + 15 more calls
- Build mockups for everyone who said yes (reuse the demo templates)
- Send them: *"Here's what your site could look like — [link]. Happy with it? I can have it live this week."*
- Keep dialling fresh leads.

### Day 4 — Follow up HARD
- Everyone who got a mockup: call them. *"Did you get a chance to look? What did you think?"*
- This is where sales are won. The money is in the follow-up, not the first contact.
- 15 more new calls.

### Day 5 — Close
- Push the warm ones to a decision. *"I can start today and have you live by [day]. Shall I get going?"*
- Take deposit (50% up front is standard and fair). **First money in the door.**

### Day 6–7 — Deliver + upsell
- Build what you sold (you've got the templates — a basic site is a day's work)
- Upsell delivered clients: Google Business setup, extra pages, photos (£50–150 each)
- Ask every happy client: *"Know anyone else who needs a site?"* — referrals are free leads.

## Rules that keep you sane
1. **Rejection is the job, not a failure.** 9 of 10 say no. The 10th pays your week.
2. **Don't discount in panic.** £399 is already cheap. Hold the price; sell the value (more customers, looks pro on Google).
3. **Always get the next step.** Never end a call without a mockup agreed or a callback time booked.
4. **Log everything.** A lead you forget to follow up is money left on the table.
5. **Cash up front.** 50% deposit before you build. Non-negotiable.

> One website. That's the whole target. Go get the first "yes."
