Good To Know

Your Questions, Answered

Still unsure about something? Call us on 01494 211802 — we're happy to talk it through.

Can you remove scratches from my car's paintwork?
In most cases, yes. Light scratches and swirl marks are removed through multi-stage machine paint correction, while deeper scratches that haven't gone through the clear coat can be significantly reduced. We'll always assess your paint honestly and tell you exactly what's achievable before we start.
How long does ceramic coating last?
It depends on the coating and how the car is cared for, but a professionally applied ceramic coating typically protects for several years. Regular maintenance washes and the occasional top-up keep the hydrophobic, glossy finish performing at its best for as long as possible.
How long does a full car detailing service take?
A maintenance valet can be same-day, while full paint correction, PPF or a ceramic coating often needs the car for a few days to allow proper preparation, application and curing time. We'll give you a clear timeframe with your quote — and our free local collection means minimal disruption to you.
Do you offer mobile car detailing services?
No — all work is carried out in our dedicated studio, where we can control the environment, lighting and dust for a truly flawless finish. Instead, we bring the convenience to you with free collection within 15 miles and arranged transport for cars further afield.
