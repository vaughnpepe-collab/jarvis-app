Our Story

Cooking Since 1998

Started by the Sharma family in a small kitchen in High Wycombe, Spice Garden has grown into one of Buckinghamshire's most loved Indian restaurants — without ever changing our values.

  • Family-owned and run for over 25 years
  • Spices imported directly from Rajasthan, India
  • All dishes prepared fresh daily — nothing frozen
  • Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options always available
  • Private dining room for up to 30 guests
