FAQ

Questions answered.

The ones we get asked every single time.

What do I need to provide? +
Your business name, a short description of what you do, your phone and email, and ideally a logo. No logo? We'll create a simple one. No photos? We source professional stock images that suit your business — no extra charge.
Is 48 hours really guaranteed? +
48 working hours from when you send us your content. We'll send you a preview link within that window. If anything delays us, we'll tell you immediately — and you don't pay until the site's done to your satisfaction.
Do I own the website? +
100%. Once we're paid, we hand over the full files. You can move it to any host you like. No lock-ins, no recurring fees unless you choose our optional hosting plan.
Will it show up on Google? +
Every site we build is SEO-ready: proper page titles, meta descriptions, fast load speed, and mobile-friendly (which Google prioritises). Full Google Business Profile setup and SEO boost is available as a £149 add-on.
What if I want changes after it's live? +
You get 30 days of free tweaks after go-live — text changes, photo swaps, colour adjustments. After that, our maintenance plan is £39/month, or we quote any bigger changes on a one-off basis.
Can I get a free mockup for my specific business? +
Yes. Just message us your business name and type. We'll put together a quick mockup of what your homepage could look like — free, no obligation. Most clients say seeing the mockup is what convinced them.
