## HOW TO FIND BUSINESSES TO EMAIL

### Method 1 — Google Maps (fastest)
1. Go to Google Maps
2. Search: `plumber [town]` / `restaurant [town]` / `salon [town]` etc.
3. Click each result and look for **"No website"** under their listing
4. Copy their phone number — most business emails are on Google or their Facebook page
5. If no email, move to WhatsApp/Facebook DM (scripts below)

### Method 2 — Yell.com
- Search your target industry + town
- Listings with no website link are your targets
- Email is often listed

### Method 3 — Facebook
- Search `[town] + [business type]` in Facebook groups or pages
- Look for businesses with no website link in their bio

### Method 4 — Instagram
- Search hashtags like `#[town]plumber`, `#[town]hairdresser` etc.
- Look for accounts with no website in bio

## TIPS FOR HIGHER REPLY RATES

1. **Personalise the demo link** — send the trades demo to plumbers, restaurant demo to restaurants, salon demo to salons. It makes it feel like you've already thought about their business specifically.

2. **Mention something specific** — "I noticed you're on Checkatrade but have no website" is 3x more likely to get a reply than a generic message.

3. **Lead with the free mockup offer** — the word "free" is your biggest conversion tool. Most people who get a mockup convert.

4. **Message in the morning** — 8:30–10am gets the best open rates for trades. Restaurants: 2–4pm (post-lunch lull). Salons: Tuesday–Thursday mornings.

5. **Volume matters** — aim for 20+ contacts per day. At 1-in-15 conversion rate, 20/day = ~1 sale per week = £499.

6. **Follow up** — 40% of deals close on follow-up. Always send a second message if no reply after 4 days.
