Our Story

Born from a love of real food.

The Golden Fork opened its doors in 2012 with a simple idea: serve brilliant food in a warm, unhurried space where people actually want to linger. Twelve years later, that idea hasn't changed.

Head Chef Marcus Reid trained in Paris and Lyon before returning to the UK with a conviction that the best British produce deserves classical technique — and that a good meal should feel like a special occasion every time.

We work with local farms and suppliers where possible, changing our menu with the seasons to keep things honest and fresh.
