# 10 — Your Trading Plan (Template)

The plan is the product of this whole course. A method you can't state in writing
is a method you can't follow under pressure. Copy this template, fill every blank,
and trade *only* what it says. If a trade isn't in the plan, it isn't a trade.

> Print it. Re-read it before every session. Update it only on a calm weekend with
> evidence from your journal — **never** mid-trade.

---

## My Trading Plan — v1 (date: ________)

### 1. Identity & limits
- **Account size:** ____________
- **Risk per trade (base):** ______ % (start 0.25–0.5%)
- **Conviction max risk (cap):** ______ % (≤ 2%)
- **Daily loss limit:** ______ % → when hit, **I disable algo trading until tomorrow.**
- **Max concurrent open risk (all positions):** ______ %
- **Markets I trade:** ____________ (start with ONE)
- **Sessions I trade (server time):** London __:__–__:__  /  NY __:__–__:__

### 2. Bias (module 02)
- **Macro thesis (one sentence):**
  _"________________________________________________ → my bias on ______ is ______."_
- **Technical confirmation:** H4 close vs. ____-EMA, and HH/HL or LH/LL structure.
- **Rule:** I take trades **only** in the bias direction. Conflict between story and
  chart = **no trade**.

### 3. Entry checklist (module 04) — ALL must be true
- [ ] H4 bias aligned (long-only or short-only)
- [ ] Inside a trading session, spread acceptable
- [ ] Liquidity **sweep** of a swing point (spike beyond + close back through)
- [ ] **Structure shift** (close beyond the opposing swing = BOS/CHoCH)
- [ ] Stop fits beyond the sweep (+ ATR buffer)
- [ ] Reward:risk to next liquidity ≥ ______ : 1  (min 3:1)
- [ ] No high-impact news in the next ____ minutes

> If any box is unticked, **I do not enter.** No exceptions, no "this one's special".

### 4. Risk & sizing (module 05)
- Stop placed at: __________________________ (beyond the sweep).
- Size = (equity × risk%) ÷ loss-per-lot at my stop. **Never a fixed lot.**
- Conviction up-size only when: ____________________________ (e.g. H4 + Daily agree),
  capped at my max-risk %.

### 5. Management (module 06)
- Move to **break-even at +____ R**.
- After +1R: ☐ trail by ATR  ☐ fixed target  ☐ scale out ____% at ____R, trail rest
- I will **not** widen a stop or add to a loser. Ever.
- Manual override is allowed ONLY for: ☐ news flatten  ☐ H4 bias flip  ☐ daily limit.

### 6. Process & review (module 07)
- I journal **every** trade: screenshots, checklist, R-result, one-line lesson.
- I review **weekly**, sorted by process score (not P&L).
- I track monthly: expectancy (R), win rate, max drawdown, **rule-adherence %**.
- I scale risk up only after a profitable, rule-adherent live sample of ≥ ____ trades.

### 7. My promises to myself
- I will lose small and often, and that is the plan working — not a failure.
- I will sit on my hands when there is no setup. **No setup is a winning day.**
- I will protect capital first, because the drawdown table (module 05) is undefeated.
- I will treat the EA as discipline, not a crystal ball, and I will keep it small.

## Pre-session checklist (tape to your monitor)

1. What's the H4 bias on my symbol today? (long-only / short-only / stand aside)
2. Any high-impact news scheduled? When? Will I flatten / pause?
3. Is my daily loss limit set and am I emotionally fit to trade?
4. Sessions/spread/algo-trading enabled and correct (server time)?
5. Am I here to **wait for my pitch**, not to "be in a trade"?

## Post-session checklist

1. Did I follow the plan on every trade? Where didn't I — and why?
2. Journaled with screenshots and R-results?
3. One lesson for tomorrow: ________________________________________
